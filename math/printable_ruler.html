📏 Printable Ruler

Print Instructions:

  • Important: Print at 100% scale (no shrink/fit to page)
  • Use regular 8.5" x 11" letter paper
  • Cut along the black border to make your ruler
  • Check accuracy: the ruler should be exactly 6 inches long

How to Read the Marks:

  • Tallest marks = Whole inches (1, 2, 3...)
  • Second tallest = Half inches (½)
  • Third tallest = Quarter inches (¼, ¾)
  • Shortest marks = Eighth inches (⅛, ⅜, ⅝, ⅞)

Mark Size Guide

Half inch (½)
