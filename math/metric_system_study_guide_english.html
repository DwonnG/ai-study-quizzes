üí° Memory Tips (Trucos para recordar)

  • Kilo = 1,000 - A kilogram is 1,000 grams, a kilometer is 1,000 meters
  • Centi = 100 - There are 100 centimeters in 1 meter
  • Milli = 1,000 tiny parts - 1,000 millimeters in a meter, 1,000 mL in a liter
  • Think about what you're measuring:
    • Can you hold it in your hand? ‚Üí Use smaller units (g, cm, mL)
    • Is it bigger than you? ‚Üí Use larger units (kg, m, L)
    • Is it a distance you'd drive? ‚Üí Use km

üîÑ Conversion Cheat Sheet

  • To go from BIGGER to SMALLER unit: MULTIPLY by 10, 100, or 1,000
  • To go from SMALLER to BIGGER unit: DIVIDE by 10, 100, or 1,000
  • Example: 3 kg ‚Üí g = 3 √ó 1,000 = 3,000 g ‚úì
  • Example: 500 mL ‚Üí L = 500 √∑ 1,000 = 0.5 L ‚úì
